Vision

To be a globally recognized institution committed to excellence in education, research, and innovation, nurturing responsible technocrats to serve society.

Mission

  • To deliver high-quality technical education through innovative curriculum and pedagogy.
  • To encourage research, innovation, and lifelong learning among students and faculty.
  • To promote ethical values, leadership skills, and societal responsibility.

Program Specific Outcomes (PSOs)

  1. Graduates will be able to apply principles of computer science and engineering to solve real-world problems.
  2. Graduates will be able to design, implement, and maintain software systems using modern engineering tools.
